Oxford English Dictionary

Oxford University Press

The Oxford English Dictionary published by Oxford University Press is a publication considered to be the first English dictionary. This dictionary began to be built in 1857 but it was not until 1884 that it was first published in the series: A New English Dictionary on Historical Principles; Founded Mainly on the Materials Collected by The Philological Society, while work on compiling the dictionary was still ongoing.
