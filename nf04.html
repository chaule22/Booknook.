Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity

Peter Attia MD, Bill Gifford

"Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity" is an insightful and comprehensive exploration of the fascinating world of longevity. The book delves into the latest scientific research, offering readers a deep understanding of the factors that contribute to a longer and healthier life. From genetics and diet to exercise and lifestyle choices, the author provides a balanced and evidence-based perspective on the art of extending one's lifespan. The writing is engaging and accessible, making complex concepts easy to grasp for both experts and the general reader. By combining scientific knowledge with practical advice, "Outlive" becomes a valuable guide for anyone interested in optimizing their well-being and embracing the journey towards a longer, more fulfilling life.
